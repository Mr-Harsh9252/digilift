What is Digilift Academy?

Digilift Academy is a modern Digital Marketing School in Delhi. We focus on providing practical training in areas of digital marketing like SEO, PPC, social media marketing, email marketing, content marketing, and many more. We aim to equip students with the practical skills to excel in the rapidly evolving digital world.

The primary Digilift Academy training center is in Delhi. We also provide online courses for people that cannot attend the classes in person. Explore the self-paced learning options offered on the website anytime.

What courses does Digilift Academy offer?

Digilift Academy has the following courses:

  • Social Media Marketing - Learn to manage a social media account by mastering content creation and advertisement running.
  • PPC Advertising - Gain in-depth knowledge of Google Ads and mastering PPC campaigns in our instructor led courses.
  • Email Marketing - Learn to design email campaigns that effectively convert leads to clients.
  • Content Marketing - Master the art of crafting content that is not only interesting but also captivates and effectively converts the audience.
