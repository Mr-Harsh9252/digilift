Privacy Policy

DigiLift Academy's comprehensive privacy policy and data protection guidelines

At Digilift Academy Digital Marketing Institute, we care about protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we gather, utilize, and protect your information when you visit our website, digiliftacademy.com, and make use of our digital marketing academy services. By engaging with Digilift Academy Training in Delhi, you are agreeing to the collection and use of your information as described here.

In order to make our services better, we gather the following kinds of information:

Personal Information:

We may gather your name, email address, phone number, and billing information when you register on our Site, sign up for our courses, or subscribe to Digilift Academy Student Reviews or newsletters.

Non-Personal Information:

We collect data such as your IP address, browser type, device information, the pages you visit, and the date and time you visit our Site to better understand how you interact with our Site, including your interest in Digilift Academy Placement or Digilift Academy Reviews.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

To improve your experience on our Site, track usage, and improve services, we may use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies. You can adjust your browser settings to turn off cookies; however, certain features of our Site may not work as intended.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following:

  • To grant you access to our courses and services, including Digilift Academy Training in Delhi.
  • To notify you concerning the account, courses, services, promotions, as well as updates relevant to our services, including details about Digilift Academy Placement.
  • To improve the user experience on our Site and modify the information to your interests, particularly information related to Digilift Academy Digital Marketing Institute.
  • To receive payments and execute requests for courses and services.
  • To provide you answers to your questions and any customer support services you require, including queries about Digilift Academy Contact Number.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your private information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising and marketing. With that said, your information may be shared in the following situations:

Service Providers:

Your private information may be shared with trusted third parties that help with the operation of the Site, payments, emails, and other business activities related to Digilift Academy Digital Marketing Institute.

Legal compliance:

Your information may be shared in the event that there is a legal requirement or there is a legal obligation, right, or property of Digilift Academy Placement that needs to be protected, or in the event of fraud.

4. Data Protection

We grant you the authority to access your personal data, modify it, or even delete it. If you wish to update or delete your information, just get in touch with us. You can also stop receiving marketing done by us by either clicking the "unsubscribe" button in the emails or by contacting us directly using the Digilift Academy Contact Number.

Our Site may have links to other websites or services that are not under our control. We are not liable for the privacy policies or information contained in those other sites. We strongly recommend that you examine the privacy policies of those sites prior to giving any personal information.

Important changes will be communicated to you via the Site, where an updated policy will be uploaded alongside a new effective date. We encourage you to check this Privacy Policy from time to time to know the ways in which we are safeguarding your information and to stay informed about Digilift Academy Student Reviews, Digilift Academy Placement, and more.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?
